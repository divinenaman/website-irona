The Initiative

The MH Covid Relief is an initiative by a team of student volunteers from Miranda House, University of Delhi. Lead verification and connecting people to the required resources has been our prime area of focus.



With the pandemic forcing many to navigate through these strange times alone and more often than not, clueless, we are setting up this fundraiser to mitigate, connect and help people in these strange times. What you can do at the moment is lend a helping hand.



The only thing that has emerged as a force combating this pandemic is the sense of community. Kindness of random strangers who put in their time, efforts and resources has been a surprising constant. And thus, this fundraiser aims to carry forward with this resilient emotion to connect the much needed monetary resources as aid for people in need during this calamatious reality. Whatever you contribute, truly makes a difference. We thank you for your support and hope that together we can work towards COVID relief to the maximum extent possible. DONATE NOW
